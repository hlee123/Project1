On this page, I will post about my trip to Chicago. I've only been to Chicago for three weeks, so I haven't been to many places, but I've veen to a few places.

(This video was taken during boat tour...)

I bought ticket for boat tour for $37, and the ticket box is located Michigan Ave, under DuSable Bridge. My ticket is Lake & River Architecture Tour to see Many Architecture at sunset and night.

Tickets prices are a little expensive but I strongly recommend them. Because through this tour, you can see a view of downtown chicago.

After the boat tour, I went to Navy Pier. I saw nice fireworks there. It was a great day.
